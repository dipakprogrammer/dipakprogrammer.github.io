About RWA

The Resident Welfare Association (RWA) in the society is formed to handle different issues and matters pertaining to its members. RWA acts as the voice of it's members and not only take care of ensuring/monitoring good maintenance of the place but also represent members when and where needed on matters related to the place. The Resident Welfare Associations take care among others, matters pertaining to its members such as administering the rules and bylaws of the association, dealing with builder for various related matters and audit/manage the funds of the association and income/expenditure of the association and maintenance charges being collected by the Builder/maintenance company.

Independence Day & Janmashtami Celebration

Dear All,
Happy to inform, we are celebrating the Independence Day and Janmashtami on 15th August 2017 in Mangalam Ananda, activities & events to be organized on 15th and 16th August are given below:
15-Aug-2017
Independence day
8:00AM: Flag Hosting (Same will be done by residents from defence background )
8.30AM to 10.00AM: Plantation of Trees in Ananda by Children living in Ananda. Each child will Plant a tree on the day.
10.00AM to 12:00PM: Recognition by RWA to all the children living in Ananda who have:
a. Scored 80% or more in their examinations of 10th and 12th in the last Academic year
b. Children who have represented District, State or National level in any sports or any other competition

Fancy dress (patriotic theme) competition for children in two age group : up to 5 years and 6 to 15 years
4:00PM: Inauguration of Library and RWA office
Janmashtami
6:30PM to 8:00PM: Cultural evening on Radha/Krishna theme
9:30PM to 12:30AM: Bhajan and Krishna Janmostav

16-Aug-2017 6:00PM to 8:30PM: Dahihandi and Nandotsav
To make this event successful all residents are requested to get in touch with your block representatives to share the list of Children for both point 1 ( with the photocopy of the certificate or mark sheet) and 2, also for fancy dress competition and cultural performance.
We request all residents to share the same maximum by 7thAugust so that the arrangements will be done on time.
"Requesting all the Block representatives to please share the same in their blocks with their names"
